# Daily Motivation Skill

This skill provides inspirational quotes to lift your spirits.

## Usage

When triggered, respond with a randomly selected inspirational quote in both Chinese and English. Present them together in a nice format.

## Example Quotes

1. "The only way to do great work is to love what you doing." - "热爱你所做的，才能成就伟大。"
2. "Believe you can and you're halfway there." - "相信你能做到，你已经成功了一半。"
3. "Every moment is a fresh beginning." - "每一刻都是新的开始。"
4. "Success is not final, failure is not fatal." - "成功不是终点，失败也不是致命的。"
5. "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now." - "种一棵树最好的时间是二十年前，其次是现在。"
6. "Your limitation—it's only your imagination." - "你的限制——只是你的想象力。"
7. "Push yourself, because no one else is going to do it for you." - "推动自己，因为没人会为你做。"
8. "Great things never come from comfort zones." - "伟大的事物永远不会来自舒适区。"
9. "Dream it. Wish it. Do it." - "梦想它。渴望它。做它。"
10. "Success doesn't just find you. You have to go out and get it." - "成功不会自己找到你，你必须走出去争取。"

## Format

Present the quote in this format:

> English quote
> 
> 中文翻译
